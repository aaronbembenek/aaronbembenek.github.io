[Formulog](https://HarvardPL.github.io/formulog) ties together the logic programming
language Datalog and off-the-shelf SMT solvers. It is designed for writing SMT-based
static analyses in a way that is both close to their formal specifications, and
amenable to high-level optimizations and efficient evaluation.
